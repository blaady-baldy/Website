Runthrough 2k20

Leaving the house before parents wake up and reaching back after they're asleep. Living on a diet of Adrak and Chai, looking like a homeless destitute in those worn out kurtas, and having attendance in every college, except yours. Par, yahi din toh baad mein yaad aayenge. Aayaam- The Dramatics Society of MAIT is delighted to present to you, the fifth edition of its Annual Street Play Event, Runthrough'20. The registrations for the preliminary round are now open. To register your play, fill out the form given below: https://forms.gle/aNtJj88fSouVh4qm6 Registrations close on 14th March at 11:59 pm. Performances toh bohot ho gayi, chalo, ek baar firse Runthrough nikaalte hain..

Curtain Call 2k20

Nothing is more real than the masks we wear to show each other who we are. The stage provides an actor with multiple masks to create one's own identity. We are delighted to present you another opportunity to discover your identity with us. Presenting to you, Curtain Call 2k20 - The annual stage play festival of Aayaam. Registration are now open and will close on 14th March, 11:59 p.m. No entries will be entertained after the deadline. To register please fill the google form given below: https://docs.google.com/…/1FAIpQLScmku95AQ-9NXz73N…/viewform
